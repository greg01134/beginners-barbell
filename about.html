About The Coach

11 years of experience, boiled down to the basics.

My Training Philosophy

Hello! I've been coaching strength and conditioning for over 11 years, and in that time, I've seen countless fitness fads come and go. The one thing that never changes? The effectiveness of basic, heavy barbell training.

I created The Beginner's Barbell because I hate seeing people walk into a gym, feel intimidated by the complex machines and fitness influencers, and turn right back around. You don't need a complicated routine to get strong and healthy. You just need consistency, good form, and the willingness to learn the "Big 3."

My philosophy is simple: Master the basics, lift with intention, and leave your ego at the door.
